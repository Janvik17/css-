Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices bread

Instructions:

1. Cook the bacon until crispy.

2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese, bacon, tomato, and another slice of cheese between the bread.

4. Grill the sandwich on a pan until golden brown on both sides.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!
